# E-commerce-Sales-Insight
In the dynamic landscape of e-commerce, understanding and harnessing sales data is paramount for strategic decision-making. The E-commerce Sales Insight Excel Dashboard is a comprehensive tool designed to provide a deep dive into the sales dynamics of your online store. This dashboard is meticulously crafted to empower stakeholders with actionable insights, allowing them to make informed decisions and drive business growth.

## Key Insights :-
1. Women are more likely to buy as compared to men (~65%).
2. Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 states (~35%).
3. Adult age group (30-49 yrs) is max contrubuting (~50%).
4. Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra chennels are max contributing (~80%).
5. Target Women customers of age group (30-49 yrs) living in Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh by showing ads/offers/coupons available on Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra.
